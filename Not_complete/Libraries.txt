Plot libraries in Python:

seaborn-specialized statistical visualisation, built on matplotlib,categorical plots ,count plots, heat maps, violin plots,scatter plots,bar plots,various colors,offer

pywaffle-categorical data vis, waffle charts, square pie charts, donut charts, unique way to plot proportions
